Head-Butt-Alotl Mob Guide

Note: This guide is based on the current game code. Some values are dynamic or randomized, so ranges are shown where needed. Screenshots are not included here, but each entry includes a short visual description.

ENEMIES / HOSTILES

1. Alien - grunt
- Health: scales from the alien base formula; lower than brute/spike, moderate overall
- Damage to player: base type damage 1, then scaled by size and current large-alien multiplier
- Rewards: same as all standard aliens, about 8 to 12 Silver and 12 XP on kill
- HP given to player: none
- Description: green alien with horned head, blocky body, belly plate, legs, and eyes

2. Alien - brute
- Health: much higher than other standard aliens due to higher hp multiplier and larger scale
- Damage to player: very high, especially after the new large-alien damage multiplier
- Rewards: about 8 to 12 Silver and 12 XP on kill
- HP given to player: none
- Description: big blue heavy alien, large body and head, thick silhouette, meant to feel like a tank

3. Alien - spike
- Health: medium-high
- Damage to player: high
- Rewards: about 8 to 12 Silver and 12 XP on kill
- HP given to player: none
- Description: pink/red alien with horned look and more dangerous silhouette

4. Alien - tiny
- Health: lowest of the alien types
- Damage to player: low, but still dangerous in groups
- Rewards: about 8 to 12 Silver and 12 XP on kill
- HP given to player: none
- Description: small yellow alien, fast-looking and compact

5. Shark
- Health: about 85 to 130 HP
- Damage to player: about 18 to 30 damage per second while in contact
- Rewards: 18 Silver and 22 XP
- HP given to player: none
- Description: gray shark with back, belly, dorsal fin, tail, jaw, eyes, and gills

6. Anglerfish
- Health: not shown in one constant line, but built as a heavier deep-water hostile with significant durability
- Damage to player: about 14 to 22 damage per second while in contact
- Rewards: 22 Silver and 28 XP
- HP given to player: none
- Description: dark deep-sea fish with a lure, jaw, fins, and a bulky head-heavy silhouette

7. Leviathan
- Health: 900 HP
- Damage to player: 100 damage
- Rewards: none currently defined in gameplay loop
- HP given to player: none
- Description: huge dark predator with long body, fin, tail, and jaw; more like a boss creature

8. Sea Urchin
- Health: about 18 to 30 HP
- Damage to player: about 4 damage per second on contact
- Rewards: none directly defined right now
- HP given to player: none
- Description: small dark urchin body with colorful glowing spines

9. Crab
- Health: about 14 to 22 HP
- Damage to player: about 3 damage per second on contact
- Rewards: none directly defined right now
- HP given to player: none
- Description: orange-red crab with shell, claws, legs, and raised eyes
